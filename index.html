What is TurboSavings?
TurboSavings is a straightforward way to make passive income. Invest your Net balance and earn daily yield based on your available APY percentage and options trading turnover for the past 30d.
Can i withdraw instantly?
Withdrawals of the TurboSavings feature function similarly to regular withdrawals: you submit a request for a specific amount, and our finance team promptly handles it. Additionally, while TurboSavings is active, Net balance remains locked for your convenience and security.
How are the payout being calculated?
Once TurboSavings is enabled, your Net balance becomes an investment that earns daily returns based on the APY percentage that can be set to Flexible or Locked, but you cannot withdraw the funds while the feature is active. An example: Your Net $1000 x APY 128% = $1280, this is your Annual income. Annual profit $1280 / 365 days = $3.50 of passive Daily income.
What is APY?
APY stands for Annual Percentage Yield. It's a way to measure how much money you can earn on a deposit or investment over the course of a year. APY helps you understand how much money you can make on your savings or investments over time. It's important to consider APY when comparing different options to find the best way to grow your money.
